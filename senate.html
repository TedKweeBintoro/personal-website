66% percent of all SGA legislation since the 2017 summer term has only changed its procedure.

Why does this matter?

The role of the UNT Student Senate is to serve the interests of the UNT undergraduate student body. However, the vast majority of what it has passed has not established any new action to benefit the student body. We need leaders in the Senate who are ready to take charge and push legislation that will actually matter- not just a resolution that'll add a line or two to the SGA By-Laws.
