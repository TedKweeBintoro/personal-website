Projects

I first picked up a cello in the summer of 6th grade. 5 years and 3 schools later, I've gained invaluable life experience, maturing both as a musician and as a student.

School

I started playing at Seven Lakes Junior High's first year orchestra as a sixth grader, moving to the Tays Junior High Chamber Orchestra a year later. I am currently a cellist in the Tompkins High School Sinfonietta Orchestra.

Extracurricular

In the 8th grade, I played in the TMEA Region 23 Philharmonic Orchestra, and in the Symphony Orchestra of the Katy Youth Symphony. As a freshman, I was in the Sinfonia Orchestra of the Houston Youth Symphony.

Solo and Ensemble

As an 8th grader, I performed the fourth movement of Haydn's "The Bird" and the first movement of Saint-Saëns' Cello Concerto No. 1 at the UIL Solo and Small Ensemble Contest, earning a Superior ranking on both. As a freshman, I performed the first movement of Kummer's first Cello Duet for the Solo and Ensemble and Katy Young Artist competitions, earning a Superior ranking in both. I also received an Excellent ranking at the Solo and Ensemble competition for my performance of the first movement of Schubert's "Arpeggione Sonata".
