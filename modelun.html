Projects

As a member of the OTHS Model UN team since 2018, I've had the opportunity to work with many talented speakers and writers, and I've learned important lessons about cooperation and hard work along the way.

BaylorMUN 2018

At the 2018 Baylor University conference, I represented the Islamic Republic of Iran in DISEC on the topic of cyberterrorism, winning an Honourable Mention.

WAMUNCC 2018

At the 2018 Westlake Academy MUN Crisis Conference, I represented Secretary of the Treasury Steven Mnuchin in the Presidential Cabinet on the topic of the situation in Syria.

HAMUN 2019

At the 2019 Houston Area MUN conference, I represented the Republic of Indonesia in DISEC on the topics of small arms and light weapons proliferation and the situation in the South China Sea.

OTHSMUN 2019

At the 2019 Tompkins High School MUN conference, I represented the United States of America, first in the Security Council on the topic of the situation in Myanmar, and later in SOCHUM on the topic of women's reproductive rights.

RiceMUN 2019

At the 2019 Rice University MUN conference, I represented the Russian Federation in SOCHUM on the topics of the protection of journalists and ending the use of child labour, winning a Best Position Paper award.
