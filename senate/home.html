Our Vision

We need to build a UNT that works for everyone—TAMS and non-TAMS students alike.

Transparency

As we've seen before, making decisions without informing people is a recipe for disaster. We need less closed-door decision-making and more opportunities to engage with our leadership. As your Senator, I'll fight to hold the UNT administration accountable.

Equality

When we win awards, get published, or even pay our tuition, it's all done through UNT. We're UNT students just like everyone else on campus. As your Senator, I'll fight to ensure we're treated like other UNT colleges.

Democracy

If we want to take back our future at TAMS, we need to have a say in how things are run. UNT has developed an undemocratic system where they decide and we listen. As your Senator, I'll fight to give every student a voice at UNT.
